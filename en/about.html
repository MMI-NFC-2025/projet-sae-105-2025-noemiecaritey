Me

My name is Noémie Caritey and I am currently a first-year student in the Multimedia and Internet Professions (MMI) degree program in Montbéliard.

Passionate about art and design, I love exploring how visual creation can convey emotions, tell a story or bring an idea to life.

Thanks to my training, I am developing diverse skills in fields such as graphic design, visual communication, digital creation and interactive design.

I enjoy experimenting, learning new techniques and finding original ways to connect aesthetics and functionality in my projects.

The Project

This website aims to highlight women scientists who have made history through their discoveries, research and determination.

Too often overshadowed by their male counterparts, these women have nevertheless advanced science and contributed essentially to understanding the world around us.

Through this project, I wanted to pay tribute to them and remind that science was not built solely thanks to men, but also thanks to these pioneers, researchers and inventors whose contributions deserve to be recognized and transmitted.

Combining aesthetics, playful aspect and educational dimension, the site offers an interactive and visual experience that invites to discover these inspiring journeys while highlighting the place of women in the scientific world.
