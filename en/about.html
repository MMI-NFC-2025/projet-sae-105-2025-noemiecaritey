About

My name is Noémie Caritey and I am currently a first-year student in the Multimedia and Internet Professions (MMI) Bachelor’s program in Montbéliard.

Passionate about art and design, I enjoy exploring how visual creation can convey emotions, tell a story, or bring an idea to life.

Thanks to my studies, I am developing a wide range of skills in fields such as graphic design, visual communication, digital creation, and interactive design.

I like experimenting, learning new techniques, and finding original ways to connect aesthetics and functionality in my projects.

The Project

This website aims to highlight women scientists who have made history through their discoveries, their research, and their determination.

Too often overshadowed by their male counterparts, these women have nevertheless advanced science and contributed in an essential way to our understanding of the world around us.

Through this project, I wanted to pay tribute to them and to remind that science was not built solely thanks to men, but also thanks to these pioneers, researchers, and inventors whose contributions deserve to be recognized and passed on.

Combining aesthetics, a playful aspect and an educational dimension, the site offers an interactive and visual experience that invites visitors to discover these inspiring journeys while highlighting the place of women in the scientific world.
