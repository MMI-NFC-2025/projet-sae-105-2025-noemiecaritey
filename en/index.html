The Matilda Effect is a phenomenon where women who contribute to the advancement of scientific research are pushed aside and have their work stolen by their male counterparts because their status as women does not grant them all the attention they should receive.

Here, to pay tribute to them and to remind that science was not built solely thanks to men, but also thanks to these pioneers, researchers and inventors whose contributions deserve to be recognized and passed on.
